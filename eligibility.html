Eligibility

A practical eligibility screen for PIs, institutions and project teams before starting the online application.

Applicant reminder: final eligibility should always be checked against the current HEC call documents and policy files before submission.

Principal Investigator

  • Faculty member at a higher education institution in Pakistan.
  • Research profile aligned with the proposed project area.
  • Able to submit through the host institution and required portal workflow.
  • Prepared to provide institutional approvals, CV, budget and supporting documents.

Host institution

  • Recognized higher education institution or degree-awarding institution where applicable.
  • ORIC or responsible institutional office available for administration.
  • Capacity to manage grant agreement, finance and reporting obligations.
  • Ability to respond during the correction window.

Project readiness check
