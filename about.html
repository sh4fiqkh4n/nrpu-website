About NRPU

Program overview, purpose and operating model for the National Research Program for Universities.

Purpose

NRPU supports high-quality scientific research by enabling university faculty to formulate research concepts and methods, build collaborative teams, and conduct projects through host higher education institutions in Pakistan.

The program is structured around competitive research grant awards administered through the host institution and its ORIC function.

Research focus

  • Basic and applied research with clear methodology.
  • Projects connected to national, societal, academic or industry-relevant needs.
  • Collaboration across senior faculty, junior researchers, students and sectoral partners where appropriate.
  • Transparent screening, review and award stages.

Information architecture

For Principal Investigators

Eligibility, scope, document requirements and submission stages are separated into short decision blocks.

For ORIC teams

Institution-level routing, correction windows and document checks are presented alongside the call timeline.

For reviewers

Proposal structure, merit progression and review sequencing are visible without requiring applicants to search multiple pages.

National Research Program for Universities

A structured call-information website for researchers, ORIC teams and institutional administrators.
