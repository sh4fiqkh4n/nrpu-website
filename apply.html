Apply

A guided online path from proposal outline preparation to final submission on the HEC research portal.

Read the current call

Start with the official NRPU introduction, SOPs, revised policy and financial rules. Download the latest templates before drafting.

Prepare proposal outline

Build a concise outline with objectives, literature rationale, methodology, team, budget and expected outcomes.

Submit through portal

Applications are submitted online through the HEC research portal according to the call guidelines.

Track correction notices

Missing or incorrect documents should be corrected before the correction window closes.

Proposal intake draft

Document checklist

  • Proposal outline / concept note
  • PI and team CVs
  • Budget and justification
  • Institutional endorsement through ORIC
  • Ethics / biosafety approvals if applicable
  • Partner letters where applicable
